## 🌟 Overview

**EventFlow AI** is India's first AI-powered client acquisition system designed specifically for paid startup meetups, business networking events, AI workshops, and high-ticket masterminds.

The system sources high-intent professionals from LinkedIn, engages them with hyper-personalized outreach, converts them into paid attendees, and tracks every rupee of revenue for event organizers.

### 🎯 Core Promise
> **Predictable paid registrations without relying on ads.**
